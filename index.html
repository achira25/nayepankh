Who we are

A student organisation
with a stubborn belief
that things can be better

NayePankh means "New Wings." We are one of India's largest student-led NGOs, run entirely by young people who believe giving back is not charity — it is responsibility.

From distributing meals outside railway stations at midnight to setting up free tuition centres in resettlement colonies, we go where the need is.

What we do

Four things.
Every single week.

01

Free Food

Hot meals at railway stations, slum areas, and disaster relief camps. No questions asked, no forms to fill.

02

Education

Free tuition, stationery, and mentorship for children who have never had an adult in their corner.

03

Clothes Drive

We collect, sort, and distribute clean clothes across communities, giving dignity back to those who have lost it.

04

Sanitary Pads

Breaking the silence around menstrual health. Free pads and open conversation — because half the population deserves basic dignity.

Data & Machine Learning

We measure what we do,
so we can do more of
what works

We applied four ML models to our outreach data to understand growth patterns, geographic distribution, and predict impact — so every rupee goes further.

Beneficiary Growth Prediction

Linear and Polynomial Regression trained on 5 years of field data. The linear model scores best on out-of-sample years — simple signals beat complexity here.

K-Means City Clustering

K=3 clusters group operational cities by population density vs. outreach reach. Cluster A cities (high-density, lower penetration) are our next priority zones.
